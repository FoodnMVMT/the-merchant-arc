How we help

Outcomes first, products second. We start by understanding your current setup — then show you the options clearly.

01

Merchant Statement Review

We analyze your current statement, calculate your true effective rate, and explain interchange, assessments, processor markup, and every monthly and incidental fee.

02

POS & Payments Setup Review

We look at whether your POS and processing are bundled, whether you can switch processors or need a new POS path, and how your terminal and gateway are configured.

03

Dual Pricing & Cash Discount

We explain dual pricing vs. cash discount, what customers see, the compliance considerations, and whether the approach actually fits your business model.

04

Debit, Credit & PIN Debit Clarity

We break down credit, signature debit, and PIN debit — and where the cost differences live — so you know who captures the savings and how it's disclosed.

05

Processor & POS Recommendation

We match your needs to the right platform, prioritizing transparent pricing, real support, and ownership of your customer relationship over a quick switch.

06

Onboarding Support

We help prepare documents, configure equipment, and coach your staff on customer-facing language — reducing the friction of any change you decide to make.

The first step

A free, no-pressure statement review

You don't need another rate quote first — you need someone to explain your current statement. Send one recent processing statement and we'll show you exactly where your money is going. If there's nothing worth changing, we'll tell you that.

Request your review

What we'll identify

  • Your true effective rate (total fees ÷ total card volume)
  • Monthly card volume, transaction count & average ticket
  • Credit vs. debit card mix
  • Interchange, dues & assessments vs. processor markup
  • PCI, gateway, batch, chargeback & equipment fees
  • Whether dual pricing or a different setup could help
